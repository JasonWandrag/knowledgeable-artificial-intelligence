KAI welcomes you


About


This is a fun project that lets you speak to your laptop, and it will reply. Please note that this is not an actual AI


This application only recognizes a handful of commands


Currently understood terms


  • Hello | Good morning | Good afternoon | Good evening
  • My name is YOUR_NAME_HERE
  • How are you? | Are you well?
  • Who are you? | What is your name? | Introduce yourself
  • Tell me a joke | Make me laugh
  • Thank you
  • I'm bored | Give me something to do | What can I do?
  • Kanye West
  • Chuck Norris

You can say any of these phrases, and KAI will respond appropriately






This project was put together to test my JS skills. I wanted to see if I could use Speech-To-Text => Understand simple phrases => generate or get reply => Text-To-Speech
